Call Center for Agricultural Technique

What is Call Center for Agricultural Technique?

Systematically providing agricultural information and technical services to farmers or clients by connecting them to technical officers or researchers and farmers or clients; and addressing their challenges on agricultural techniques.

Vision

Promoting agricultural production, productivity, product quality and safety of farmers or clients in order to make them more accessible to the market and reduce climate-changed impact.

Benefits of Call Center for Agricultural Technique

Farmers and clients obtain agricultural information, techniques and solution effectively and efficiently from technical officers without payment. Technical officers share experiences and appropriate agricultural techniques to farmers and clients.

Who can access Call Center for Agricultural Technique ?

Are the farmers, researchers, extension agents, or clients who concern about agricultural technique.
